Visa Requirements by Country

Here’s a general overview of what you need to apply:

Malaysia

  • Offer Letter from University
  • Passport Copy (Minimum 18 Months Validity)
  • No Bank Statement Required
  • Health Check (After arrival)

Australia

  • Offer Letter (COE)
  • Valid Passport
  • Financial Proof (Bank Statement)
  • English Proficiency Test (IELTS/PTE)
  • Genuine Temporary Entrant (GTE) Statement

USA

  • I-20 Form from University
  • Valid Passport
  • Financial Support Proof (Bank Statement, Sponsor Letter)
  • SEVIS Fee Payment
  • F1 Visa Interview

Finland

  • University Acceptance Letter
  • Valid Passport
  • Financial Proof (6,720 EUR minimum)
  • Insurance, Accommodation Details

Denmark

  • Acceptance Letter
  • Valid Passport
  • Proof of Sufficient Funds
  • Residence Permit Application

UK

  • CAS (Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies)
  • Valid Passport
  • Financial Evidence (28 Days Bank Statement)
  • TB Test Certificate (Depending on country)

China

  • JW202/JW201 Form
  • Admission Letter from University
  • Valid Passport
  • Financial Proof
  • Health Certificate
