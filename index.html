About Us

Knowledge Bridge connects students with world-class education opportunities across Malaysia, Australia, USA, Finland, Denmark, UK, China, and more. We offer full support for university applications, visas, accommodations, and scholarships.

Visa Requirements

  • Malaysia: No bank statement needed
  • Australia: Bank statement, IELTS 6.0+
  • USA: Strong bank statement, SAT/GED/IELTS
  • UK: IELTS/TOEFL, Bank statement
  • Finland: Proof of Funds, Admission Letter
  • Denmark: Proof of Funds, Residence Permit
  • China: Admission Letter, JW202 Form
